Burned Interior Cedar, Tree 93#

Not far from the Erratic Trail. This 30' snag shows the usual pattern: mostly bare of bark, a burned interior cleft, heartwood gone, perhaps died in the ~250 year fire, standing and rotting away slowly ever since.
  • DBH: 3'3"
  • First observed: 30 January 2021
  • Observer: Paul Shannon
  • Burn: extensive in interior core

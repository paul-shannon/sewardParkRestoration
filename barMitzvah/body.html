The Bar Mitzvah Site (11 July 2020)

Some of the forest regulars encoutered self-identified Bar Mitzvah celebrants in early July. We later discovered that members of this group, no doubt with good intentions, but lacking our usual sense of the fragility of the forest, and the need to leave it undisturbed:
  • cleared, cut and marked a fresh trail
  • created a 20' diameter clearing, which included the cutting of a large, mature hazelnut shrub
  • carried in and distributed gravel to mark the clearing's circumference
  • held a large celebration

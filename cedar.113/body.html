Two Burned Bark Cedars, Trees 113, 114

These two cedars seem to be anomalies - in that the other cedars collected to date have burned, sometimes burned-out cores. These two have only surface bark with burn: a stripe about one foot wide, about six feet tall. They are located within a dozen feet of each other at the south junction of the sqebeqsed and Windfall trails.
  • DBH: 3'7" and 3'9"
  • First observed: 28 February 2021
  • Observer: Paul, Leo, Cleek
  • Burn: south side, similar vertical strip, on surface

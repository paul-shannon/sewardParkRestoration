High Ground Die-off

This area (about 5 acres) in enclosed by the sqebeqsed and Windfall Trails, occupying the highest ground in the forest. The southern quarter - our focus here - is dominated by large sword ferns with few other understory plants.

In January I found that about 3% of the ferns in these five acres were dead, all of them intermittently distributed, no regions of contiguous death.

Now, October 2nd 2020, and with sorrow, I report that contiguous die-off has appeared, showing what seem to be the classic signs of the die-off.

It is always possible that this is a transient phenomenon, strictly limited to the small area picture below, and not the first appearance of concentrated die-off. Time will tell.

Social Trail #001

This trail may have been present for a few years, but became especially obvious in June 2020. It runs east from the sqebeqsed for a couple of hundred feet.

Our plan is to close the trail at its entrance with a big obstacle woven of branches, brush and CWD, leaving the interior of the trail to recover on its own, tracking progress here.

Invasive Ivy, Andrews Bay Cliffs, Site #4

This site is the fourth of four identified on the Andrews Bay, Blakeley Sandstone Cliffs, counting from the southwest (closer to the Pottery Studio) toward the northeast (approaching Kingfisher Point).

This site is quite large.

  • First observed: 11 January 2021

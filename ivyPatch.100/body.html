Small Ivy Patch, East Side #

Discovered from the lower loop road, a small patch almost contiguous with the big hatchery site. One sturdy holly tree in the middle of this patch, previously cut and resprouted, suggests the area may have been cleared before, maybe as part of ivyOUT in the early 2000s.
  • area: 900 sq. ft.
  • First observed: 20 February 2021
  • Observer: Paul Shannon
  • Apparently cleared as part of IvyOUT project
  • 2021 February 20: some ivy pulled
